Headlight Care Guide

Your headlights now have a fresh start. Follow these simple steps to keep them crystal clear for the full five-year warranty and beyond.

  1. First 2 hours

    Avoid water or moisture. The UV sealant is still curing, so keep sprinklers, rain, and washing to a minimum during this window.

  2. First 24 hours

    Skip the automatic car wash until the next day. Hand washing with a soft mitt and pH-neutral soap is okay after the first 24 hours.

  3. Weekly washing

    Wash by hand or touch-free. Avoid stiff brushes or harsh chemicals that could dull the sealant.

  4. Quarterly check-in

    Wipe the headlights with a clean microfiber towel and mild soap. If you notice new haze, contact us right away—your warranty covers it.

Quick Do’s and Don’ts

A handy list to screenshot or keep on your phone.

Do

  • Park in the shade when possible.
  • Use gentle, non-abrasive cleaners.
  • Wax nearby paint as usual—just avoid the lens.

Don’t

  • Use harsh solvents or bug/tar removers directly on the lens.
  • Machine polish the headlights—call us if you need help.
  • Ignore new haze—it’s covered under the five-year warranty.
