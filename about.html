About The Lightsmith

Dedicated to restoring clarity, safety, and style to headlights throughout Caldwell and the Treasure Valley.

At The Lightsmith, we believe your headlights should shine as bright as the day your vehicle rolled off the lot. Based in Caldwell, Idaho, we specialize in premium headlight restoration that dramatically improves nighttime visibility and revitalizes the look of your car.

We follow a proven process that removes yellowing, oxidation, and scratching before sealing lenses with a durable UV protectant. Every restoration is backed by a five-year warranty, so you have confidence that the clarity will last.

Owner-operator Jack brings meticulous attention to detail and mobile convenience straight to your driveway. With honest pricing, flexible scheduling, and friendly service, The Lightsmith keeps your vehicle ready for the road ahead.
