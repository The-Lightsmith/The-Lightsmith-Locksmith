    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to the questions drivers ask before booking.

    What does the service cost?

    Our full restoration is $100 per vehicle. That includes sanding, polishing, the Headlight Magics UV sealant, and a five-year satisfaction guarantee.

    How soon can I drive after the appointment?

    You can drive immediately. Avoid water for the first hour and skip the car wash for 24 hours to let the sealant fully cure.

    How should I care for the headlights afterward?

    Use touch-free or gentle hand washes and avoid harsh abrasives. We’ve written a short care guide with all the tips.
